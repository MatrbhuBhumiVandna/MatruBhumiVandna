Recent Events

Our latest activities and programs

Janta Raja Natak 2023

May 5-10, 2023

Our annual Janta Raja Natak was a grand success with over 5000 attendees across 6 days. The historical play depicted the life and struggles of our freedom fighters, inspiring the audience with patriotic fervor.

The event raised significant funds for the temple construction and brought the community together in celebration of our cultural heritage.

Martyrs' Day Tribute

March 23, 2023

On the anniversary of Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru's martyrdom, we organized a special tribute program featuring speeches, patriotic songs, and a candlelight vigil.

The event was attended by local school children who performed skits depicting the bravery of our freedom fighters.

Gurukul Inauguration

January 14, 2023

We inaugurated the first phase of our modern Gurukul with Vedic chanting and traditional rituals. The Gurukul will offer a blend of traditional Vedic education and modern curriculum.

The inaugural session featured lectures on the importance of preserving our ancient knowledge systems while adapting to contemporary needs.
