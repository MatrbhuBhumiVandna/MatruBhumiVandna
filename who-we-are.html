Who We Are

Founded in 2017, Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust started with a dedicated team of nine visionaries, united by a common goal - to make Morbi proud by building the world's biggest Bharat Mata Temple and reviving the Gurukul/Vedic education system.

Our Journey

2017 - The trust was founded with a mission to construct a grand temple for Bharat Mata.

2019 - Organized Janta Raja Natak, a historical play, to raise donations for the temple.

March 23rd (Every Year) - Conduct special programs to honor the sacrifices of Indian patriots.

Present & Future - Continuing our mission to spread Sanatan Dharma, Indian culture, and nationalism through the Bharat Mata Temple and Gurukul education system.

Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust stands as a pillar of spirituality, nationalism, and social responsibility, working tirelessly to create a self-reliant and culturally strong India.
